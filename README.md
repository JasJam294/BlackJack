# Blackjack
Browser-based Blackjack game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

## Access the Game Here
https://jasjam294.github.io/BlackJack/ 

## How to Play
- Press "Deal" to begin the game
- Press "Hit" to draw another card
- Press "Stay" to keep your hand and let the dealer play
- Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over 21 to win
- An "Ace" + any card with a value of 10 is considered a Blackjack - instant win!

## Extra Features
- Animated card dealing
- Dealer AI draws until hand of 17
- Win/Loss/Tie tracking
- Win streak badge
